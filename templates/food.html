Food of Pune

Discover the Flavorful Essence of Pune's Culinary Delights

Cuisine

Mastani

A luscious and indulgent milkshake-like beverage from Pune, enriched with thickened milk, ice cream, and an array of toppings, offering a delightful treat for dessert lovers.

Misal Pav

A spicy and flavorful Maharashtrian dish featuring sprouted lentils cooked in a fiery gravy, served with pav (bread) and topped with crunchy farsan, providing a burst of flavors and textures in every bite.

Bakarwadi

A crispy and savory snack hailing from Maharashtra, consisting of gram flour dough spirals filled with a spicy and tangy mixture, deep-fried to golden perfection, perfect for satisfying savory cravings.

Pithla Bhakari

A traditional Maharashtrian dish comprising a thick and savory gram flour curry (pithla) served with unleavened bread (bhakari), offering a wholesome and comforting meal option.

Sabudana Khichadi

A popular fasting dish made with soaked tapioca pearls (sabudana), peanuts, potatoes, and aromatic spices, creating a flavorful and nutritious meal often enjoyed during religious festivals or fasting days.

Puranpoli

A sweet flatbread filled with a delicious mixture of cooked lentils (puran) and jaggery, flavored with cardamom and nutmeg, making it a delightful dessert or snack enjoyed during festive occasions in Maharashtra.

Street Food

Keema Pav at Goodluck Cafe

A delightful dish consisting of spicy minced meat (keema) served with soft pav bread, renowned for its rich flavors and hearty appeal.

Chicken Jumbo Burger at Burger - East Street

A mouthwatering combination of succulent chicken patty, fresh vegetables, and creamy sauces sandwiched between soft burger buns, offering a satisfying meal on-the-go.

Mysore Masala Dosa at Vaishali

A South Indian classic, the Mysore Masala Dosa at Vaishali is a thin, crispy rice and lentil crepe filled with a spicy and tangy potato mixture, served with coconut chutney and sambar for a flavorful breakfast or snack.

Jumbo Vada Pav at Garden Vada Pav Centre

A beloved street food staple, the Jumbo Vada Pav features a spiced potato patty (vada) sandwiched between soft pav bread, served with chutneys for an explosion of flavors with every bite.

Chicken Biriyani at Tiranga Bhuvan Kothrud

A fragrant and aromatic rice dish cooked with succulent pieces of chicken, fragrant spices, and herbs, delivering a burst of flavors that tantalize the taste buds.

Delhi Chaat at Jaishankar Pani Puriwala

A medley of crispy puris filled with spicy and tangy chutneys, yogurt, and a variety of toppings like sev, onions, and coriander, offering a burst of flavors and textures in every bite.

Cheesecake at German Bakery

A creamy and decadent dessert featuring a velvety smooth cheesecake filling atop a buttery biscuit crust, perfect for satisfying your sweet cravings.

Puneri Misal Pav at Bedekar Misal

A spicy and flavorsome Maharashtrian dish consisting of sprouted lentils cooked in a fiery gravy, topped with crunchy farsan and served with soft pav bread, known for its fiery flavor and hearty appeal.

Sandwich at Supreme Sandwich

A classic comfort food, the sandwich at Supreme Sandwich is a delightful combination of fresh vegetables, meats, cheese, and spreads layered between slices of bread, offering a satisfying and wholesome meal option.

Burger at Camp Burger

A delicious burger featuring a juicy beef or veggie patty, fresh lettuce, tomatoes, onions, cheese, and sauces, sandwiched between soft burger buns for a satisfying and indulgent meal.
